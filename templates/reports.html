Reports

Filter your sales data by shop, location, and date range. Export to Excel or PDF when ready.

ℹ How to generate a report
  1. Shops — choose a specific shop abbreviation or leave as All Shops. The location list below updates automatically.
  2. Shop Location — narrow down to a single location, or keep All Locations.
  3. Period — pick a preset rolling window (e.g. Last 30 Days), or tick Custom Date Range to enter exact From/To dates.
  4. Click Generate Report to view the results table with transaction count, total quantity, and total revenue.
  5. Click Export Report to download as Excel, PDF, or both.

💡 Demo tip: to see the pre-loaded sample data, tick Custom Date Range and enter From: 2026-05-01  /  To: 2026-05-31, then click Generate Report.
